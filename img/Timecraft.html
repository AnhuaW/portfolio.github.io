    Project Overview

    Timecraft is a web-based app designed for students and professionals working from home. it aims to help enhance time management and assis users in building a productive routine

    Goals and Objectives

    To create an intuitive, easy to use time managment tool that caters to the specific needs of remote students and workers.

    Problem

    Challenge

    To first understand and then address the unique time management challenges faced by remote workers and students, such as distraction, procrastination, and inefficient task management.

    User Needs

    A reliable, user-friendly app that integrates task management with effective time tracking and minimization of distractions

    Research

    Interview

    We conducted semi-structerued interviews on 8 participants with 4 work from home professionals and 4 students with remote expereinces to learn more about thier needs and frustrations.

    Competivie Analysis

    We also decided to do a competitive analysis on 4 different time managment tools as they emerged during our interviews in order to have a better understanding of the strengths and weaknesses of exisitng tools.

    Key Findings

    After collecting and analyzing the data from interview as well as our competitive analysis, we discovered that there is a high demand for an all-in-one solution for:

    1. Task Tracking
    2. Event Management
    3. Focus Enhancement
    4. Performance Analysis
