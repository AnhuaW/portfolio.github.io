    Project Overview

    During the pandemic, many people expereinced a sudden change to their day-to-day life, speicifically, many had to switch from in-person to virutal working and learning enviroment. People who work and learn virtually from home face a unique set of challenges.

    Timecraft is a web-based time mangement app designed and tailerd for students and professionals working from home. It aims to help users stay organized and assist them in building a productive routine.

    Problem Statement

    How might we address the unique time management challenges faced by remote workers and students with our design effectively?

    Goals and Objectives

    1. Allow users to add and keep track of various tasks and events.
    2. Help users to minimize disractions while they stay focused on a task.
    3. Encourage users to use the app often to build a productive routine.
    4. Keep the design simple to ensure the tool is intuitive and easy to use.

    1. Task Tracking
    2. Event Management
    3. Focus Enhancement
