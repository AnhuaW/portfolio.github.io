    Overview

    Project Overview

    Project Quest started as a case-study of classic RPG games like Franken and Undertale with a emphasis on origianl narrative and novel mechanics.

    Problem Statement

    How might we address the unique time management challenges faced by remote workers and students with our design effectively?

    Goals and Objectives

    1. Allow users to add and keep track of various tasks and events.
    2. Help users to minimize disractions while they stay focused on a task.
    3. Encourage users to use the app often to build a productive routine.
    4. Keep the design simple to ensure the tool is intuitive and easy to use.

    Research

    Interview

    We conducted semi-structerued interviews with 8 participants consisting 4 work from home professionals and 4 students with remote expereinces to learn more about thier needs and frustrations.

    Competivie Analysis

    We also decided to do a competitive analysis on 4 different time managment tools as they emerged during our interviews in order to have a better understanding of the strengths and weaknesses of exisitng tools.

    Key Findings

    After collecting and analyzing the data from interview as well as our competitive analysis, we discovered that there is a high demand for an all-in-one solution for:

    1. Task Tracking
    2. Event Management
    3. Focus Enhancement
    4. Performance Analysis

    Reflection

    Key Learnings

    It was my first 0-1 design that allowed me to practice with foundamental research and design skills. I had gained valuable insights on user-centric data collection and analysis through conducting interview, usability testing and affinity mapping. For me, the experience hilighted the importance of data-driven design process and equipped me with essential knowledge on creating an accessible experience.

    Next Steps

    1. Design a schedule builder for users to easily block off time and for better visualization of time allocations.
    2. Implement a monthly view of My Schedule for easy lookup of upcoming tasks and events.
    3. Work on the details on analysis and achievement, specifically the userflow of the points and badge system.
